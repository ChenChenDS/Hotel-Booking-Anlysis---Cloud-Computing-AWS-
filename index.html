Hotel Booking Analysis



Introduction



The hotel and hospitality sector caters to millions of travellers everyday. Hotel companies always want to optimize their revenue by accurately predict demand and price. You must have experience of booking hotels. As a customers, you must want to optimize your spending. For the same room, you must prefer to pay less. This hotel booking analysis can help us explore some interesting outcomes both useful for hotel holders and customers.



Data Source



Resort Hotel and City Hotel data in 2015-2017
32 variables
119391 observations

Data Preprocessing



Drop columns with missing value over 90%
Drop rows with missing
Label encoding for categorical value



Hotel type



  • 2/3 of our data are city hotel and 1/3 are resort hotel.



    • Hotel loyalty



  • Resort hotels loyalty member is twice as the loyalty member for city hotels.



    • Hotel booking numbers by month



  • City hotels have two peaks, May and October.
  • In winter, city hotels have the lowest booking number.
  • City hotels booking amount in highest season is twice as much as in lowest season.
  • Resort hotels booking amount fluctuation is small.



    • Hotel room rate by month



  • City hotels room price in May is the highest.
  • Resort hotels room rate shows huge increase in the summer vacation period.



    • Booking cancelation rate by month



  • City hotels cancelation rate is around 40% yearly round.
  • Resort hotels cancelation rate is higher in summer.

    •  

    Correlation between price and number



  • There is positive correlation between hotel room rate and booking number.

    AWS Architecture





    Summarize



  • In summer vacation, travel in cities is more cost-effective than a trip to the beach or somewhere else with resort hotels. If you want to travel to resort hotels, May and June are better. Also, the November room rate is the lowest. But there are always hurricanes in November.
  • In summer, room cancelation rate for resort hotel is high, so you may have chance to find some last minute rate.
  • When you travel to the city in May or October, book your hotel early, the room occupancy at these months are high.
  • If you are a hotel holder, maybe tightening the cancellation policy for resort hotels in the summer period, will help to reduce the cancelation rate. For city hotels, maybe give more benefits for loyalty customers can attract more loyalty customers.
